Welcome to Makeospace

Makeospace is a fast growing turnkey interior services provider company in Nagpur to deliver end to end interior solutions to residential and commercial interiors.

Makeospace is the complete space planning execution by professional designers providing turnkey solutions to any type of projects and takes care of everything with our services in Nagpur.

We undertake take responsibility of each and every space will be planned and designed professionally . Expert team of designers and vendors utilize every space effectively and ergonomically to gift the best interior experience. We offer from the basic lay out designs to finishing touches on every space with perfectionism

Our Services

Residential Interiors

Residential interior designs reflect the values and culture followed by an individual. The colors, designs, patterns, almost every element of the interiors gives the sneak peak of a person’s personality. It is the way of living that is depicted by the interior design of a home.

Commercial Interiors

Commercial interior design, it is about the interior designs of commercial spaces like Hospitality, restaurants, retail stores and many other public spaces. It is the duty of the commercial interior designers to add furniture, finishings, fixtures, drapery, etc. to complement and complete the look as per the original design goals.

Office Interiors

Office spaces, we may be looking for an organized space with easy accessibility. This professional space needs to be designed to fuel productivity and keep the work environment energized throughout the day.
